Nice Rendering for Gnuplot Images

Oct 15th, 2011

If you use the default output of gnuplot eps or png the rendering is somehow quite bad. No antialiasing and small fonts, but there are better alternatives.

set terminal pngcairo font “Gill Sans,9” linewidth 4 rounded size 1600,1200

Try it out and you will be surprised
